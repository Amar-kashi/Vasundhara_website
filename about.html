Our Mission & Goal

  • We at Vasundhara aim to equip the underprivileged children with education and skills to enable them to become a part of mainstream society.
  • We are also committed in skill development of girls and young women in and around Sarojini Nagar, Lucknow to make them financially independent.
  • VASUNDHARA - AN INITIATIVE is strongly committed to the vision of providing access to formal education free of cost to the underprivileged children of slums of Mavaiyya and nearby areas in Lucknow.
  • We provide formal schooling to these children from pre-nursery to class 5th and then we ensure continuity of their higher education by providing all support in the form of tuition fees, school uniform, books and stationery till post-graduation level.
  • Our goal is to achieve complete literacy for all underprivileged children of slum areas and become a centre for women empowerment.
